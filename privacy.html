Privacy Policy – Advanced Media Tool

Last Updated: 09/12/2025

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used, and protected when using the Advanced Media Tool application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the following user-provided data:

  • Username
  • Password (securely stored and never shared)
  • User ID (automatically generated)

No other personal, financial, location-based, or sensitive data is collected from the user.

2. How We Use the Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Creating and managing user accounts
  • Enabling login to the app

The collected data is not used for:

  • Advertising
  • Marketing
  • Analytics
  • User tracking

3. Data Sharing

We do not share any collected data with third parties. No external analytics, advertising networks, or third-party partners receive your data.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. Passwords are stored securely using encryption techniques.

5. No Tracking

The app does not use any tracking technologies or collect device identifiers.
